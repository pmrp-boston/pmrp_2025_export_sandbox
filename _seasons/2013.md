title: 2013 Season
---
*   [Our performance at Arisia in January 2013](/events/arisia13) was a reprise of “The Day the Earth Stood Still”, originally adapted for Lux Radio Theatre.
*   In April our [Spring Sci-Fi Spectacular](/events/sss13) featured another episode of Red Shift, “Crisis of the Cuddlykins”, along with our original adaptation of the Atomic-age monster thriller “Them”.
*   In July we presented an adaptation of the classic Sherlock Holmes story [“The Sign of Four”](http://pmrp.org/events/srt13) for our Summer Radio Mystery Theatre series.
*   In October [our Hallowe’en show](/events/notld) featured of “Night of the Living Dead” in our original adaptation and our version of the Suspense! episode “Ghost Hunt”.
*   In December we presented our first Christmas-themed show, [The Big Broadcast of 1962: A Byfar Christmas Carol](/events/bb1962).
