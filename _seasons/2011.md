*   Our 2011 season started with [Doctor Who: The Starship of Madness](/events/starshipofmadness) at Arisia 2011 in which the Doctor encounters horrifying forces from hidden dimensions, with encore presentations in March.
*   In July of 2011 we presented [Summer Radio Theatre ’11](/events/srt11), featuring our versions of “The Do-Nothing Detective” from Black Jack Justice, “Junkyard” from X Minus One, and the classic Escape! episode “Three Skeleton Key”.
*   In October 2011 we joined with several other radio-theater groups around the country to present [Sleepy Hollow: The Ride Across America](/projects/sleepyhollow11) as part of [the Big Broadcast of 1954](/events/bb1954).
