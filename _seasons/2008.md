*   [Our performance at Arisia in January 2008](/events/arisia08) was an episode of our original series Red Shift: Interplanetary Do-Gooder, titled “Havoc over Holowood”.
*   In October 2008 we presented [Tomes of Terror 3](/events/tomes3), featuring our recreations of a Hallowe’en episode of The Baby Snooks Show and “The Tell-Tale Heart” as adapted for Nightfall and “Reynardine”, an original adaptation of Athan Y. Chilton's short story “The Ballad of Reynardine”.
*   And [we closed out the year](/events/firstnight09) with another original Red Shift episode, “Countdown to Chaos”, and a reprise of our recreation of “Chicken Heart” for Boston’s First Night celebration.
