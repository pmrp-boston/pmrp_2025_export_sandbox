*   [Our performance at Arisia in January 2009](/events/arisia09) was an episode of our original series Red Shift: Interplanetary Do-Gooder, titled “Dance of the Duplicates”. We were accompanied by the cast of [Second Shift](http://www.secondshiftpodcast.com/) performing “Dreams”, and participated in a panel on radio drama in New England.
*   In October 2009 we went back to the Golden Age of Radio, to the Rooftop Gardens Auditorium of the old Putnam-Moore Department Store in downtown Boston for [the Big Broadcast of 1938](/events/bb1938), including the true story of the Martian Invasion of Boston during the War of the Worlds!
