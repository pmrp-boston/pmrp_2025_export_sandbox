*   [Our performance at Arisia in January 2014](/events/arisia14) was a gender-swapped adaptation of the 1960s Star Trek episode “The Trouble with Tribbles” featuring a harried Captain Jane T. Kirk and her science officer, Ms. Spock. There was [an encore performance](/events/tribbles) in May.
*   [Summer Radio Mystery Theatre ’14: Super Sleuths](/events/srt14) (July 2014) featured Hercule Poirot in “The Case of the Careless Victim”, Lady Molly of Scotland Yard in “The Ninescore Mystery”, and Sherlock Holmes—and Jack the Ripper—in “Sherlock Holmes and the Whitechapel Murders”.
*   Our Hallowe’en show was the Edgar Allan Poe-themed anthology [Tomes of Terror: Nevermore](/events/tomes5) (October 2014), featuring original adaptations of the Poe short stories “The Masque of the Red Death”, “The Fall of the House of Usher”, and “The Cask of Amontillado”, as well as a dramatic reading of Poe’s famous poem “The Raven”.
