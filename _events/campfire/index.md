**Performances:** Six performances from October 21-22 and 27-29 at
[Responsible Grace Church, 204 Elm Street, Somerville](#location).
Performances will be at 8pm, except that Saturday the 22d will have both
a 2pm matinée and an 8pm evening performance.  
![](/sites/default/files/CampfireTalesLongSmall.jpg)  
<span class="lightbluebold">The Post-Meridian Radio Players’</span>
annual Halloween show brings you a collection of chilling urban legends
and campfire stories this October\!

This tapestry of terror is woven from short plays by **Amy
Bennett-Zendzian**, **Michael Lin**, **J. Deschene**, **Tom Russell**,
**Thomas J. Misuraca**, **Kevin Kordis**, and **Mare Freed**

<big><big>[You can buy digital audio of <cite>Campfire Tales</cite> on
Bandcamp\!](https://pmrp.bandcamp.com/album/tomes-of-terror-campfire-tales)</big></big>

Tomes of Terror: Campfire Tales was performed on October 21-22 and
27-29.
