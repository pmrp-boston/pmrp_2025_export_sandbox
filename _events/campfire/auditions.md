Our annual Hallowe’en show for 2016, [<cite>Tomes of Terror: Campfire
Tales</cite>](/events/campfire), will be an anthology of urban legends,
ghost stories, and other creepy campfire stories, with a shared ensemble
cast, woven together with interstitial dialogue and presented as stories
told by campers around a campfire.

This show has both Foley and voice positions available\! Voice parts
will consist of an ensemble with actors performing multiple parts
throughout the show.

Auditions will be on **Monday August 22nd at 7pm, 8pm and 9pm** and then
**Tuesday August 23rd at 7pm, 8pm and 9pm**. Callbacks, if needed, will
be on Thursday August 25th.

Auditions will be at Responsible Grace Church, 204 Elm St. in
Somerville.

Tomes of Terror: Campfire Tales will be performed on October 21-22 and
27-29.

**PLEASE NOTE:** The Post-Meridian Radio Players is a community theater
group and is run by volunteers. Our performers are not paid.

## How to Audition

1.  Think a little bit about what you’re interested in — ensemble voice
    roles are available (see below), but also live sound-effects
    performance, tech crew, and various other things.
2.  Then go to [**our audition documents
    folder**](https://drive.google.com/folderview?id=0Bz1RWNejvIxdSFB6MzZpbFVLQjQ&usp=sharing)
    on Google Drive. There you will find a paper audition form and
    rehearsal availability grid which you should print out, fill out,
    and bring with you to your audition. Closer to the auditions we’ll
    also post audition sides
    ([definition](http://pmrptest.aq.org/glossary#sides)) there, which
    you’ll probably want to look at ahead of time,.
3.  Then, [**click here to sign up for an audition
    appointment**](http://goo.gl/forms/moOIYpGkBBdRZAg02), fill out the
    form, and submit it.
4.  If you have submitted the form correctly and one of the time slots
    you requested is still available, you will get email confirming your
    audition appointment. (This is sent by a human being who has a day
    job, so don't worry if it doesn't come right away. But if it doesn’t
    come within a day or so, contact us at auditions@pmrp.org. You can
    also use that address to contact us if you have trouble submitting
    the form.)
5.  Then print out (or download to your tablet) the audition sides from
    [our Google Drive Audition Docs
    folder](https://drive.google.com/folderview?id=0Bz1RWNejvIxdSFB6MzZpbFVLQjQ&usp=sharing).
    You’ll want to look over and/or rehearse them before your audition,
    and it will be very helpful if you can bring your copy to the
    audition (although we’ll have a few extra copies printed out).
6.  Then, show up for your audition at the scheduled time\! Good luck
    and have fun\!

### Some Things to Know

  - The audition process involves one form that you fill out online (the
    appointment form) and two paper forms which you should print out,
    complete, and bring with you to the audition. They’re not
    interchangeable. (The paper forms are [on Google
    Drive](https://drive.google.com/folderview?id=0Bz1RWNejvIxdSFB6MzZpbFVLQjQ&usp=sharing).)
  - If you show up for an audition without the paper forms (or the
    sides), we’ll probably have a few extra copies printed out and you
    can fill out the forms there. However, it saves time and trouble and
    makes the process go more smoothly if you can bring completed forms
    with you. (It also helps demonstrate to the directors how
    responsible and resourceful you are\!)
  - **Walk-ins** are welcome, but **people with appointments will take
    priority**. If there are no-shows during a given hour, we will fill
    those slots with walk-ins. Any remaining walk-ins will be auditioned
    at 10pm. It helps the directors tremendously if you sign up in
    advance, though.

## Available Roles

This is an ensemble show; voice actors will be portraying campers
telling stories around a campfire, and then also portraying the
characters in each story. So voice actors will be performing in multiple
segments, and also in the interstitial scenes.  

## Foley Artists (Live Sound Effects)

If you’re interested in being a Foley performer and making the live
sound effects, there will be a Foley interview at the time of your
audition slot. You can absolutely both audition for a voice role and
express interest in being a Foley artist (although you can only *do* one
or the other, since voice actors and Foley artists are performing
simultaneously). Just let us know on your form what you want to be
considered for: voice, Foley, or both.
