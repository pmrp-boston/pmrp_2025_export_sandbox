Come audition for [our original full-length adaptation of that paragon
of Christmastime cheer, <cite>Santa Claus Conquers the
Martians</cite>](/events/santa2019), back for its third year\! Most of
our actors are returning to their roles this year, but are auditioning
to fill two roles. Read on to find out more and then [click here to sign
up for an audition slot](https://forms.gle/5MdokDhgwJY14U5J7).

There’s more information about the show itself on [its event
page](/events/santa2019).

**PLEASE NOTE:** The Post-Meridian Radio Players is a community theater
group and is run by volunteers. Our performers are not paid.

-----

## How to Audition

Note: Most of the roles have already been cast with returning
voice-actors. We have two roles available. (Read below for a description
of them.)

1.  Read below about the available roles.
2.  [**Click here to sign up for an audition
    appointment**](https://forms.gle/5MdokDhgwJY14U5J7), fill out the
    form, and submit it.
3.  If you have submitted the form correctly and one of the time slots
    you requested is still available, you will get email confirming your
    audition appointment. (This is sent by a human being who has a day
    job, so don't worry if it doesn't come right away. But if it doesn’t
    come within a day or so, contact us at info@pmrp.org. You can also
    use that address to contact us if you have trouble submitting the
    form.)
4.  Then go to [**our audition documents
    folder**](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1v3GMxfR8vfT7z1GKZIxBpEnIx9jdjp3H?usp=sharing)
    on Google Drive. There you will find a paper audition form and
    rehearsal availability grid which you should print out, fill out,
    and bring with you to your audition. (If you forget, we’ll have
    spares for you to fill out at the audition.) It won’t necessarily
    prevent you getting cast if you have conflicts on some rehearsal
    dates.
5.  Then you’ll probably want to study and prepare the audition sides,
    also posted in [our audition documents
    folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1v3GMxfR8vfT7z1GKZIxBpEnIx9jdjp3H?usp=sharing)
6.  Then, show up for your audition at the scheduled time\! Good luck
    and have fun\!

### Some Things to Know

  - The audition process involves one form that you fill out online (the
    appointment form) and two paper forms (not available just yet) which
    you should print out, complete, and bring with you to the audition.
    They’re not interchangeable.
  - If you show up for an audition without the paper forms (or the
    sides), we’ll probably have a few extra copies printed out and you
    can fill out the forms there. However, it saves time and trouble and
    makes the process go more smoothly if you can bring completed forms
    with you. (It also helps demonstrate to the directors how
    responsible and resourceful you are\!)
  - **Walk-ins** are welcome, but **people with appointments will take
    priority**. If there are no-shows during a given hour, we will fill
    those slots with walk-ins. Any remaining walk-ins will be auditioned
    at 10pm. It helps the directors tremendously if you sign up in
    advance, though.

## Available Roles

We have two roles available:

  - **Hargo** is a Martian officer in the service of Kimar, king of
    Mars. (Hargo can be any gender.) Hargo comes with several other
    Martians to Earth to find Santa Claus, and gets mixed up in a plot.
  - **Mrs. Claus** is the long-suffering but not especially patient wife
    of the Jolly Old Elf himself. She has the misfortune to be in the
    wrong place at the wrong time when the Martians arrive, but makes
    some new friends, and speaks to a world anxious for Santa's safety.
    She will be played as a woman but may be played by a voice actor of
    any gender.
