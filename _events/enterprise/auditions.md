*Looking to audition for our Summer Mystery performance, “Mrs. Hudson
Presents”? [See this page\!](/events/srt19/auditions) You can audition
for both shows, but they’re separate auditions with separate time slots
and signup procedures.*

**Update:** [Audition
sides](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1m46wnpvIMwI-39KlDOmXgApiO5vuGlkT/view?usp=sharing)
have been posted. **(Update: We’ve had a report the link didn’t work for
everybody. It should now.)**

We are looking for an actor to play the role of Ms. Spock, the
half-Vulcan first officer serving alongside Captain Jane T. Kirk aboard
the U.S.S. <cite>Enterprise</cite>, in a reprise of our gender-swapped
adaptation of the <cite>Star Trek</cite> original-series episode “The
Enterprise Incident”, in which Ms. Spock finds her loyalties tested when
the <cite>Enterprise</cite> ventures into Romulan space.

(The other roles are already cast; only the actor playing Ms. Spock was
unable to reprise her role.)

**PLEASE NOTE:** The Post-Meridian Radio Players is a community theater
group and is run by volunteers. Our performers are not paid.

-----

## How to Audition

To sign up for an audition slot, email Caitlin at
my.london.sun@gmail.com and let her know which audition slot you prefer
(7:00, 7:30, 8:00, or 8:30). Then come **to the basement** Boston Brunch
Church on Tuesday May 14, at your chosen time slot for your audition.
**There will be auditions for another show at that time on the first
floor** so make sure you come downstairs for the <cite>Star Trek</cite>
auditions rather than accidentally auditioning for <cite>Mrs. Hudson
Presents</cite> instead\! (Of course, you’re welcome to audition for
both shows, but there are two separate signup procedures and separate
audition timeslots.) You’ll want to look over [the audition sides
**(FIXED
LINK)**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1m46wnpvIMwI-39KlDOmXgApiO5vuGlkT/view?usp=sharing)
before your audition.
