On August 9th and 10th, the Post-Meridian Radio Players will reprise the
Gender-Swapped <cite>Star Trek</cite>: episode we performed at the
Arisia science-fiction convention in January, “The Enterprise Incident”.
In our adaptation of this Original Series episode Ms. Spock finds her
loyalties tested when the <cite>Enterprise</cite> ventures into Romulan
space, and the crew of the <cite>Enterprise</cite> find themselves at
the mercy of a Romulan fleet led by a male commander.

-----

## Performance Dates

Friday, August 9, 2018 @ 8:00pm  
Saturday, August 10, 2018 @ 2:00pm (matinée)  
Saturday, August 10, 2018 @ 8:00pm

There will be a talkback immediately following Friday night’s
performance for those who wish to discuss the show and the adaptation.

All three performances will be at [Unity Somerville](#location) on
College Ave near Davis Square\!
