For Halloween 2025, the Post Meridian Radio Players are proud to present
**“The War of the Worlds and Other Invasions”**, three radio-drama
adaptations of classic H. G. Wells stories\! [Want to be a part of it?
Audition signups are now open\!](/events/invasions/auditions)

In Ron N. Butler’s adaptation of **“The Man Who Could Work Miracles”**,
directed by Jaimie Carlson, a skeptic discovers by accident that his
wishes come true, with hilarious consequences, and tries to figure out
what to do with his newfound powers.

In Joanie Brittingham’s adaptation of **“The Sea Lady”**, directed by
Jay Sekora, a mermaid comes ashore in Newport, Rhode Island, and
attempts to find her place in high society.

And in David McDowell Blue’s new adaptation of Wells’ classic, **“The
War of the Worlds”**, directed by Mare Freed, invaders from Mars attempt
to conquer Earth, whose inhabitants have to try to survive as best they
can in the face of the invaders’ superior technology.

These audio plays will be presented on stage in the style of old-time
radio with actors in front of microphones and sound effects performed
live by our talented Foley artists\!
