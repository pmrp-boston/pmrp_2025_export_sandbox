Our Spring 2024 show will feature three thrilling tales of adventure,
**The Most Dangerous Game**, an original adaptation of Richard Connell's
classic short story, the first episode of a new fantasy-adventure
pastiche **Townies and Dragons: Barrel Full of Peril**, and the pirate
adventure **The Bonnie Tales of Captain Bellamy: Song of Trickery**.

These three plays will be presented on stage in the style of old-time
radio with actors at microphones and sound effects performed live by our
talented Foley artists\!  

## The Most Dangerous Game

*Written by Jaimie Carlson. Directed by Jonathan Mendoza with assistant
director Jaimie Carlson.*  
In Jaimie Carlson’s original gender-swapped radio-drama adaptation of
the 1924 short story by Richard Connell, a big-game hunter washes ashore
on a mysterious island and winds up in another hunt — but this time as
prey\!

## Townies and Dragons: Barrel Full of Peril

*Written and directed by Michael McAfee*  
In a land of high fantasy, four friends just want to get some drinks
after work. But in a world of might and magic, adventure can happen at
any time\! This is the first episode in an original series.

## The Bonnie Tales of Captain Bellamy: Song of Trickery

*Written by Robert J. LeBlanc and directed by Francine Davis.*  
Navigating the waves of life, love, and adventure, Bonnie Captain Bonny
Bellamy and her dastardly crew on the Seawitch are the terror of the
seas, but a danger to themselves\! In this premiere episode, Captain
Bellamy learns she has slipped in the bounty rankings, so she decides to
go after the rich bounty already claimed by her rival Captain Black Jack
Malone. Can she deceive the notorious Black Jack? “The Bonnie Tales of
Captain Bellamy: Song of Trickery” was first performed in 2022 in
Portland, Maine, as part of [Balderdash
Academy](https://www.balderdashacademy.com/)’s <cite>On the Air\!</cite>
series.
