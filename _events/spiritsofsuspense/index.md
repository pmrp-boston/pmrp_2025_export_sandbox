PMRP is proud to present **three tales of Suspense and Terror**,
resurrected from the airwaves of the long dead past.

First will be the dread countdown of <cite>Suspense</cite>’s **“Three
O’Clock,”** based on the original short story by Cornell Woollrich.
Join us as a murderer’s plot goes horribly awry while he is forced to
watch… and wait.

Then we present the dread horror of Lucille Fletcher’s screenplay **“The
Furnished Floor.”** Is the man upstairs just eccentric or does, just
perhaps, the spirit of the beloved dead walk above?

Finally we present the <cite>Escape</cite> presentation of **“The
Birds,”** based off the original Daphne du Maurier short story. Birds
of a feather flock together… for blood\!

All three classic old-time radio programs will be presented on stage in
the style of old-time radio with actors in front of microphones and
sound effects performed live by our talented Foley artists\!

Performances are the last two weekends of October. There will be a
costume contest with prizes on Saturday Oct. 29th\!
