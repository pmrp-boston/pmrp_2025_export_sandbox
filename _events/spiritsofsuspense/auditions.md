**Auditions:** August 29th and 30th, 2022.

**Performances:** Six performances from October 20-30, 2022.  
**Location:** [Boston Brunch Church, 204 Elm Street,

-----

Our Halloween show for 2022 will be [Tomes of Terror: Spirits of
Suspense](/events/spiritsofsuspense), presenting three stories from the
classic radio-drama shows <cite>Suspense\!</cite> and
<cite>Escape</cite>:

  - <cite>Suspense</cite>’s **“Three O’Clock,”** based on the original
    short story by Cornell Woollrich,
  - Lucille Fletcher’s radioplay **“The Furnished Floor,”** also from
    <cite>Suspense</cite>, and
  - <cite>Escape</cite>’s adaptation of **“The Birds,”** based off the
    original Daphne du Maurier short story.

All three will be presented on stage in the style of old-time radio with
actors in front of microphones and sound effects performed live by our
talented Foley artists\! See [the show’s
page](/events/spiritsofsuspense) for more information.

Auditions will be **Monday, August 29, and Tuesday, August 30,** at
[Boston Brunch Church](#location) in Somerville. Audition slots each
night are 7pm, 8pm, and 9pm.

A list of roles will be posted soon, and audition sides will be posted
before auditions. Please **scroll down to learn how to sign up for a

Parts will be available for both voice and Foley roles. (Some crew
positions may be available as well.)

There will be six performances from **October 20-30, 2022** — evening

**IMPORTANT:** Since performers will be in close proximity on stage and

**PLEASE NOTE:** The Post-Meridian Radio Players is a community theater
group and is run by volunteers. Our performers are not paid.

## How to Audition

### Now

1.  First, [**click here to sign up for an audition
    appointment**](https://forms.gle/KKorF3CLFQjqL17GA), fill out the
    form, and submit it.
2.  If you have submitted the form correctly and one of the time slots
    you requested is still available, you will get email confirming your
    audition appointment. (This is sent by a human being who has a day
    job, so don't worry if it doesn't come right away. But if it doesn’t
    come within a day or so, contact us at auditions@pmrp.org. You can
    also use that address to contact us if you have trouble submitting
    the form.)

### Before your audition

There will be a bunch of documents posted in [our Audition Documents
folder on Google
Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1vzhuiKDdsseiqljB9lGJy6J8dMJLaROL?usp=sharing):

  - a paper audition form (there already, and different from the online
    signup form\!), and
  - a schedule grid (there already),

both of which you should print out and fill out before your rehearsal
slot if possible,

  - plot summaries and lists of roles available for the three segments
    (just posted),

which you'll want to look over as you think about what roles you’d like
to audition for, and

  - audition sides for each of the three segments (two of which are
    already up in the “Sides” folder [on Google
    Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1vzhuiKDdsseiqljB9lGJy6J8dMJLaROL?usp=sharing))

which you'll probably want to print out and practice before your
audition. (All these have now been posted.)

Note that in addition to voice roles, there are also positions for
sound-effects performance, tech crew, and various other things.

### Some Things to Know

  - The audition process involves [one form that you fill out
    online](https://forms.gle/KKorF3CLFQjqL17GA) (the appointment form)
    and two paper forms which you should print out, complete, and bring
    with you to the audition. They’re not interchangeable. (The paper
    forms are [on Google
    Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1vzhuiKDdsseiqljB9lGJy6J8dMJLaROL?usp=sharing).)
  - If you show up for an audition without the paper forms (or the
    sides), we’ll probably have a few extra copies printed out and you
    can fill out the forms there. However, it saves time and trouble and
    makes the process go more smoothly if you can bring completed forms
    with you. (It also helps demonstrate to the directors how
    responsible and resourceful you are\!)
  - **Walk-ins** are welcome, but **people with appointments will take
    priority**. If there are no-shows during a given hour, we will fill
    those slots with walk-ins. Any remaining walk-ins will be auditioned
    at 10pm. It helps the directors tremendously if you sign up in
    advance, though.
