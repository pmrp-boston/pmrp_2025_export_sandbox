This Halloween the Post-Meridian Radio Players are proud to present
[<cite>The Van Helsing Chronicles</cite>](/events/vanhelsing)\! Famed
monster hunter Professor Abra Van Helsing hosts and narrates two tales
of Gothic horror in which she eventually also plays a role. We shall be
presenting two stories, original adaptations of Richard Marsh's **The
Beetle** and Bram Stoker's **Dracula**.

### <span class="smallcaps">The Beetle</span>

**Based on the Richard Marsh novel  
Adapted by Chris Chiampa  
Directed by Amy Bennett-Zendzian with Assistant Director Rashi Verma**  
A mysterious shape-changing entity with the power to hypnotize and
control its victims seeks revenge on Paul Lessingham, a Member of
Parliament. Richard Marsh’s novel <cite>The Beetle: A Mystery</cite> was
more popular than the better-remembered <cite>Dracula</cite> when it
came out.

Adapted and Directed by Elizabeth Salazar**

comes to England to find new victims and conquer new territory—and of
the struggle by Abra Van Helsing and her companions to defeat him.
