## A Byfar Christmas Carol  
<small>December 19th–21st, 2013<small>

![The Big Broadcast of 1962](/sites/default/files/bb1962_header.jpg)  
An evening of holiday entertainment even Scrooge couldn't say “Bah,
humbug” to\!

-----

Join us this December as <span class="lightbluebold">The Post-Meridian
Radio Players</span> proudly present our grandest show of the year:
<cite>The Big Broadcast of 1962: A Byfar Christmas Carol\!</cite>

Yes folks, this time around Frank Cyrano and the gang are learning the
true meaning of the holidays in their own unique way. We'll have all of
the classic comedy from the Byfar crew and the Chowder House gang. Live
music, retro candies and soda and everything you could want in one
family-friendly show.

Tickets will be $20 and $15 for Students/Seniors.
