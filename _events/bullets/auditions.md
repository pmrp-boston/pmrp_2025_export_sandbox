For summer 2025, the Post Meridian Radio Players are proud to present
[“Bullets over Boston”](/events/bullets), two radio-drama adaptations
of Dashiell Hammett mysteries\! See [the show’s page](/events/bullets)
for more information about the adaptations.

Read on for information about auditions and the form to sign up for an
audition slot\!

Auditions will be Monday, May 12th, and and Tuesday, May 13th,
2025,</span> at [Guild Church](/venues/204elm) in Somerville. **Scroll
down to learn how to sign up for a slot and audition.**

Parts will be available for both voice and Foley roles.

There will be six performances at the end of July and the beginning of
August.

Performances may all be at the same church as auditions, or the second
weekend might be at a different venue. (We're awaiting a quote.) Exact
dates on the second weekend depend on whether it's at the church or not
but there will be six performances and the first performance will be
Friday, July 25th.

**IMPORTANT:** Since performers will be in close proximity on stage and
at rehearsals, we are requiring performers to be fully vaccinated
against COVID-19.

**PLEASE NOTE:** The Post-Meridian Radio Players is a community theater
group and is run by volunteers. Our performers are not paid.

## How to Audition

### Now

1.  [**click here to sign up for an audition
    appointment**](https://forms.gle/FwqyxM9wXQkYN7tBA), fill out the
    form, and submit it.
2.  If you have submitted the form correctly and one of the time slots
    you requested is still available, you will get email confirming your
    audition appointment. (This is sent by a human being who has a day
    job, so don't worry if it doesn't come right away. But if it doesn’t
    come within a day or so, contact us at auditions@pmrp.org. You can
    also use that address to contact us if you have trouble submitting
    the form.)

### Before your audition

There are a bunch of documents posted in [our Audition Documents folder
on Google
Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/13iZhZF97yjCF4EkeihZnQzSkaElaUCsR?usp=drive_link).
The following paper forms and documents are available there:

  - a paper audition form (different from the online signup form\!), and
  - a schedule grid,
  - synopses and character descriptions for the two radio plays  
  - audition sides for each of the two segments

<!-- end list -->

1.  After you’ve requested your audition slot, please print out (or
    download to your tablet) the audition sides from [our Google Drive
    Audition Docs
    folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/13iZhZF97yjCF4EkeihZnQzSkaElaUCsR?usp=drive_link).
    You’ll want to look over and/or rehearse them before your audition,
    and it will be very helpful if you can bring your copy to the
    audition (although we’ll have a few extra copies printed out).
2.  Then go back to [**our audition documents
    folder**](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/13iZhZF97yjCF4EkeihZnQzSkaElaUCsR?usp=drive_link)
    on Google Drive. There you will find a paper audition form and
    rehearsal availability grid which you should print out, fill out,
    and bring with you to your audition.
3.  Then, show up for your audition at the scheduled time\! Please bring
    your filled-out paper audition form and availability schedule. Good
    luck and have fun\!

### Some Things to Know

  - For this show, voice rehearsals will generally be on **Mondays**,
    with some Foley rehearsals on **Tuesdays**.
  - The audition process involves [one form that you fill out
    online](https://forms.gle/FwqyxM9wXQkYN7tBA) (the appointment form)
    and two paper forms which you should print out, complete, and bring
    with you to the audition. They’re not interchangeable. (The paper
    forms are [on Google
    Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/13iZhZF97yjCF4EkeihZnQzSkaElaUCsR?usp=drive_link).)
  - If you show up for an audition without the paper forms (or the
    sides), we’ll probably have a few extra copies printed out and you
    can fill out the forms there. However, it saves time and trouble and
    makes the process go more smoothly if you can bring completed forms
    with you. (It also helps demonstrate to the directors how
    responsible and resourceful you are\!)
  - **Walk-ins** are welcome, but **people with appointments will take
    priority**. If there are no-shows during a given hour, we will fill
    those slots with walk-ins. Any remaining walk-ins will be auditioned
    at 10pm. It helps the directors tremendously if you sign up in
    advance, though.
