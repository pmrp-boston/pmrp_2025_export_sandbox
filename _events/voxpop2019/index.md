<big><big>On Friday, August 23, 2019, PMRP reprised our original
adaptation of “The Cask of Amontillado” at the Somerville Media Center’s
pop-up performance space [VOX
POP](https://www.somervillemedia.org/voxpop/) at 431 Artisan Way in
Assembly Square, Somerville. The performance was at 7:00pm and lasted
about an hour (including a Q-and-A session following the performance).
[Their calendar of events](https://www.somervillemedia.org/voxpop/) had
more details.</big></big>

<big><big>This performance was broacast and streamed live on [Somerville
Cable Access Television](https://www.somervillemedia.org/scatv/), and
it’s now [on YouTube
here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JdQNbfhfWd4&feature=youtu.be), or
you can watch it below.</big></big>

<big><big>Naomi Ibasitas directed Mare Freed’s adaptation, which
transplants Poe’s classic tale to the Roaring Twenties. Freed originally
directed her own adaptation for PMRP as part of [<cite>Tomes of Terror:
Nevermore</cite>](/events/tomes5) in 2014, and Ibasitas first directed
it as part of Fort Point Theatre Channel’s
[<cite>Senses</cite>](http://www.fortpointtheatrechannel.org/senses)
series on Halloween night in 2018.</big></big>
