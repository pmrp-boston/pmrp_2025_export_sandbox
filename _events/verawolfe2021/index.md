![](/sites/default/files/WolfeIcon.png)The Post-Meridian Radio Players
are proud to present [The Summer of Mystery](/events/summerofmystery)\!
Three free Zoom performances, the last weekends in June, July and
August\!

First up is **Vera Wolfe** in **The Case of the Slaughtered Mrs.
Claus**\! Someone is killing women dressed up as Mrs. Claus, and it’s up
to Vera Wolfe and her right hand woman Audrey Goodwin to save Mrs. Claus
before Christmas\!
