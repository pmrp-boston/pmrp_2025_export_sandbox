The Post-Meridian Radio Players are proud to present [The Summer of
Mystery](/events/summerofmystery)\! Three free Zoom performances,
[streaming on our YouTube
channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/PostMeridianRadio/videos?view=2&live_view=502)
the last weekends in June, July and August\!

First up is **Vera Wolfe** in [**The Case of the Slaughtered Mrs.
Claus**](/events/verawolfe2021)\! Someone is killing women dressed up as
Mrs. Claus, and it’s up to Vera Wolfe and her right hand woman Audrey
Goodwin to save Mrs. Claus before Christmas\!

In July we have **Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson** in [**The Adventure
of the Bruce-Partington Plans**](/events/holmes2021), one of only two
adventures to feature Sherlock’s older, smarter brother Mycroft. Holmes
and Watson investigate the theft of the plans of England’s greatest
naval weapon, the Bruce-Partington submarine\! The fate of England
itself hangs in the balance\!

In August, **Lady Molly and Mary** return to the PMRP Summer Mysteries
to investigate [**a gruesome murder at Fordwyche
Castle**](/events/ladymolly2021) in a new adaptation of the Baroness
Orczy classic from Jeremy Holstein and Liz Salazar\!
