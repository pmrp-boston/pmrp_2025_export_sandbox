On July 18th, for **one night only** the PMRP will reprise [our Arisia
2025 Gender Swapped Star Trek performance of, "The
Menagerie"](/events/arisia25).

In "The Menagerie," after hearing of an accident involving her former
Captain, Christina Pike, Ms. Spock commandeers the Enterprise to return
to the site of a past mission for mysterious reasons.

[Program](https://pmrp-program.netlify.app/?show=menagerie)

## Venue

</a>  
We're excited to perform at this new-to-us location\! **The George
Dilboy VFW Post is wheelchair accessible** and features its own parking
lot (with overflow parking available)—no circling Davis Square looking
for street parking\! Alcohol and soft drinks will be available for
purchase, as well as snacks.

We have [a page about this venue, with a map](/venues/dilboyvfw) or you
can [see it on Google Maps
here](https://maps.app.goo.gl/abc628WjpYkEC54BA).
