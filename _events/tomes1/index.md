## Tomes of Terror

-----

![Tomes of Terror II](/sites/default/files/tomes1-lglogo.jpg)

### SHHHH\!

**THE POST-MERIDIAN RADIO PLAYERS** present their first “staged radio
thrillogy”, *Tomes of Terror\!*  
Come see how the programs that entertained America for over 30 years
were made. Actors with scripts in-hand and live sound effects artists
will make you laugh, shiver and scream—all in good fun, though\!

-----

## Featuring

**Gildersleeve’s Halloween Party:** A wonderful episode of the classic
Golden Age comedy series <cite>[Fibber McGee &
Molly](http://www.geocities.com/Hollywood/Hills/5284/)</cite> from 1939,
featuring the McGee’s popular neighbor, Throckmorton P. Gildersleeve,
and many of the regular characters from Wistful Vista.

**The Maid’s Bell:**  An adaptation of one of [Edith
Wharton](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edith_Wharton)’s best ghost
stories, “[The Lady Maid’s
Bell](http://gaslight.mtroyal.ab.ca/Ladymaid.htm)”, written for [CBC
Radio](http://www.cbc.ca/programguide/schedule/dailySchedule.jsp?network=CBC%20Radio%20One)’s
infamous [<cite>Nightfall</cite>](http://www.Nightfall-25.com) horror
series in 1982 by playwright/actor/director [John
Douglas](http://library.vicu.utoronto.ca/special/F46fonds.htm).

**The Monkey’s Paw:**  Perhaps one of the greatest of the classic horror
tales, ["The Monkey’s Paw"](http://gaslight.mtroyal.ab.ca/mnkyspaw.htm)
has rarely been rendered in audio format. Penned by [W. W.
Jacobs](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/W._W._Jacobs) in 1902, this
adaptation was written for *Nightfall* by [Len
Peterson](http://www.canadiantheatre.com/dict.pl?term=Len%20Peterson),
who has been called “Canada’s most prolific and versatile playwright”,
in 1980.
