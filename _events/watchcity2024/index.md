**Performance:**  
Saturday, May 11, 2024, at 8:30pm, as part of the [Watch City Steampunk
Festival](https://www.watchcityfestival.com/).

-----

![](/sites/default/files/RockItAlice-transparent-240x343.png)On
Saturday, May 11, PMRP performed at the [Watch City Steampunk
Festival](https://www.watchcityfestival.com/) in Waltham, MA\! We
performed <cite>Alice in Wonderland</cite> at 1:00 PM at the Waltham
Public Library (735 Main St. Waltham, MA 02451).

Like the rest of the Watch City Steampunk Festival, our performance was
free and open to the public\!

This was a shorter version of our original adaptation of Lewis Carroll's
classic tale of nonsense and whimsy, [first performed in spring of
2016](/events/alice).

The show was originally adapted by Mindy Klenoff and Jeremy Holstein,
and this reprise was directed by Kim Dauber.
