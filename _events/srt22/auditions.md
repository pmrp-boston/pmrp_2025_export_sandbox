**Auditions:** Monday, May 23 and Tuesday, May 24, 2022  
(with callbacks on Thursday, May 26, if needed)  
**Performances:** Four performances July 21-23, 2022  
**Location:** [Boston Brunch Church (formerly Responsible Grace), 204
Elm Street, Somerville](#location) (for auditions as well as
performances).

-----

The PMRP Summer Mysteries **return** with **Riddle of the Super
Sleuths**\!

We perform three mystery audio dramas live, featuring three detectives
(or their villains\!)

**Arsène Lupin** returns in the new adaptation by Michael Lin, **The
Seven of Hearts**\!

**Doctor Watson** and <span>Inspector Lestrade</span> investigate a
locked-room mystery for Sherlock Holmes in **The Adventure of the
Sleeping Cardinal**, an original play by Jeremy Holstein\!

And our third radio play is an original mystery by Phoebe Roberts, **The
Lost Days of Mrs. Neele**\!

The performance is **hosted by** Sherlock Holmes and Doctor Watson\!

Auditions will be **Monday, May 23 and Tuesday, May 24** at [Boston
Brunch Church](#location) in Somerville. [Here’s the list of available
roles, with plot summaries for the three
segments](/events/srt22/auditions/roles), and please **scroll down to
learn how to sign up for a slot and audition.**

Parts will be available for both voice and Foley roles.

There will be four performances on **July 21-23, 2022 — evening
performances on all three days, with a matinée as well on Saturday the
23d**.

**IMPORTANT:** Since performers will be in close proximity on stage and
at rehearsals, we are requiring performers to be fully vaccinated
against COVID-19.

## How to Audition

### Now

1.  Read over [the plot summaries and list of roles
    available](/events/srt22/auditions/roles) and think a little bit
    about what you’re interested in — the voice roles listed there are
    available, but there also positions for sound-effects performance,
    tech crew, and various other things.
2.  Then, [**click here to sign up for an audition
    appointment**](https://forms.gle/dC1tkp4nuv57CX1o8), fill out the
    form, and submit it.
3.  If you have submitted the form correctly and one of the time slots
    you requested is still available, you will get email confirming your
    audition appointment. (This is sent by a human being who has a day
    job, so don't worry if it doesn't come right away. But if it doesn’t
    come within a day or so, contact us at auditions@pmrp.org. You can
    also use that address to contact us if you have trouble submitting
    the form.)

### Before your audition

There are a bunch of documents posted in [our Audition Documents folder
on Google
Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1ez2c1pCWpju06YiATHt-daFVpFWZic4T?usp=sharing):

  - a paper audition form (different from the online signup form\!), and
  - a schedule grid,

both of which you should print out and fill out before your rehearsal
slot if possible, and

  - audition sides for each of the three segments

which you'll probably want to print out and practice before your
audition. (All these have now been posted. They're all available in both
PDF format and Word or Google Docs format.)

### Some Things to Know

  - The audition process involves [one form that you fill out
    online](https://forms.gle/dC1tkp4nuv57CX1o8) (the appointment form)
    and two paper forms which you should print out, complete, and bring
    with you to the audition. They’re not interchangeable. (The paper
    forms are are not up yet, but they will be [on Google
    Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1ez2c1pCWpju06YiATHt-daFVpFWZic4T?usp=sharing).)
  - If you show up for an audition without the paper forms (or the
    sides), we’ll probably have a few extra copies printed out and you
    can fill out the forms there. However, it saves time and trouble and
    makes the process go more smoothly if you can bring completed forms
    with you. (It also helps demonstrate to the directors how
    responsible and resourceful you are\!)
  - **Walk-ins** are welcome, but **people with appointments will take
    priority**. If there are no-shows during a given hour, we will fill
    those slots with walk-ins. Any remaining walk-ins will be auditioned
    at 10pm. It helps the directors tremendously if you sign up in
    advance, though.
