## Get tickets for [A Study in Scarlet](/events/srt23)\!

Join us for our 2023 summer mystery show, [A Study in
Scarlet](/events/srt23)\!

Come see our original adaptation of the very first Sherlock Holmes
adventure at one (or more\!) of our six performances. (For more about
the show, [see here](/events/srt23).)  

### Performances:

**Fri, July 21, 2023**, at 8:00pm  
**Sat, July 22, 2023**, at 2:00pm (matinee)  
**Sat, July 22, 2023**, at 8:00pm (evening)  
**Thu, July 27, 2023**, at 8:00pm (pay what you can)  
**Fri, July 28, 2023**, at 8:00pm  
**Sat, July 29, 2023**, at 8:00pm

Note that there are both a matinée and an evening show on the first
Saturday, July 22nd, and that Thursday the 27th is pay-what-you-can
night.

All performances will be at [Boston Brunch Church, 204 Elm Street,
Somerville, MA](/venues/204elm). (See [that link](/venues/204elm) for a
map.)
