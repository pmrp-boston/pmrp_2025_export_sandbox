The PMRP Summer Mysteries **return** with **A Study in Scarlet**, the
very first Sherlock Holmes adventure\!

A murder has been committed\! The body has no wounds, yet the room is
covered in blood. Scotland Yard sends for Sherlock Holmes and Doctor
Watson\! “There’s the scarlet thread of murder running through the
colorless skein of life,” says Sherlock Holmes, “and our duty is to
unravel
it.”<!-- You can <a href='/auditions'>learn how to audition and sign up for an audition slot</a> on <a href='/auditions'>our Auditions page</a>, or check back here towards the end of May for more information about the performances! -->
