![](/sites/default/files/arisia09-lglogo.jpg)IT'S GO TIME\!
<span class="lightbluebold">THE POST-MERIDIAN RADIO PLAYERS</span>
return to [Boston's largest sci-fi convention](http://2009.arisia.com)
with another live episode of their original sci-fi comedy, which pays
homage to the pulp sci-fi serials of the 1930s and 40s. Join our
heroes—Red, Lumpy, Doc Alberts and Penny Parker, Girl Reporter—as they
encounter double the trouble while in pursuit of a team of would-be
Do-Gooders, only to encounter Red's arch-nemesis, Violet Spektra\!

-----

(The **Arisia Ultraviolet Network**'s afternoon of radio drama begins at
2:00 PM with a special performance by the cast of the Parsec
Award-nominated fantasy series, <cite>[Second
Shift](http://www.secondshiftpodcast.com)</cite>, followed by our own
<cite>Red Shift: Interplanetary Do-Gooder</cite> at 3:00 PM, and a panel
discussion and Q\&A session on Audio Theater production in New England
at 4:00 PM.)

-----
