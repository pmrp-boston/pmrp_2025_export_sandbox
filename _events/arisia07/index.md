**An Arisia premiere\!** Live, original sci-fi radio drama performed
right before your very eyes and ears\! Join the stalwart Red Shift, his
mechanic Lumpy and their Earthling companions in this homage to the pulp
radio and film serials of the 1930’s and 40’s.
