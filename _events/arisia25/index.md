At [the 2025 Arisia science-fiction
convention](https://2025.arisia.org/), the Post-Meridian Radio Players
presented our gender-swapped look at the classic <cite>Star Trek</cite>
episode “The Menagerie”, directed and adapted by Liz Salazar from the
original script by Norman Spinrad. (For information on Arisia, go to
https://www.arisia.org/.)

In "The Menagerie," after hearing of an accident involving her former
Captain, Christina Pike, Ms. Spock commandeers the Enterprise to return
to the site of a past mission for mysterious reasons.  

## Performances

There was one Arisia performance on Saturday, January 18, 2025, at 5 PM
at The Westin Boston Seaport.

[A reprisal performance will occur on July 18th,
2025.](events/menagerie)
