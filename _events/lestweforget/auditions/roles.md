These are the voice roles available for [PMRP’s production <cite>Lest We
Forget</cite>](/events/lestweforget) (including two episodes of
<cite>Lest We Forget: The American Dream</cite> and an original
adaptation of the adventures of War Nurse and her Commandos in “Double
Deception”). Go to [the Auditions page](/events/lestweforget/auditions)
to learn how to audition, and about the other positions we have besides
voice acting. Actors of all ethnicities, genders and body types are
encouraged to audition.

## Roles for “Lest We Forget: Blow That Whistle”

(Most actors will probably also perform in “Hey Cabbie”.)

|                   |                                                        |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| ANNOUNCER         | Show announcer, possibly spring show host              |
| NARRATOR          | Second-person voice who puts you in Doc’s shoes        |
| JOHN “DOC” STUART | A friendly mailman who falls for hateful rhetoric      |
| HARVEY JACKSON    | Xenophobic leader of a local hate group, the Guardians |
| HELEN STUART      | Doc’s daughter, late teens or early 20s                |
| ED DAWSON         | Shoe shop owner, long-time acquaintance of Doc’s       |
| ANDY SMITH        | Young man interested in Helen                          |
| MRS. PETERS       | Resident on Doc’s mail route                           |
| SNYDER            | Guardians member who’s run afoul of the group          |
| CROWD             | Hate group members                                     |
