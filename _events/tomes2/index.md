## Tomes of Terror II

-----

![Tomes of Terror II](/sites/default/files/tomes2-lglogo.jpg)**SILENCE,
PLEASE\!**  
Once again, <span class="lightblue">**THE POST-MERIDIAN RADIO
PLAYERS</span>** bring you laughs, thrills and chills with their annual
"staged radio thrillogy", <cite>Tomes of Terror</cite>\!  
Close your eyes and hear actors, teams of sound effects artists, a live
musical accompanist, complex digital audio effects and the humor of a
unique host as they combine to paint vivid images in your mind\!  
</span>

-----

## Featuring

**Halloween Party**: An episode of the classic Golden Age comedy series
<cite>[Our Miss
Brooks](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Our_Miss_Brooks)</cite> from 1949,
featuring Constance Brooks, a teacher at Madison High School, her
explosive principal Osgood Conklin, and an assortment of students
looking for a place to hold their Halloween party. Written by Al Lewis.

**Carmilla**: Based on the creepy
[novella](http://www.sff.net/people/DoyleMacdonald/l_carmil.htm) by [J.
Sheridan LeFanu](http://mural.uv.es/franqui/engmain.html).  Performance
adaptation by Renée Johnson, based on the 1981
[<cite>Nightfall</cite>](http://www.Nightfall-25.com) episode by [John
Douglas](http://library.vicu.utoronto.ca/special/F46fonds.htm) and
[Graham Pomeroy](http://www.accommodationsincanada.com/prespen.html).

**The Stone Ship**: One of [William Hope
Hodgson](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Hope_Hodgson)'s many
frightening sea tales, this adaptation was written for
<cite>Nightfall</cite> by [Len
Peterson](http://www.canadiantheatre.com/dict.pl?term=Len%20Peterson) in
1980.
