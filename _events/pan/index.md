![](/sites/default/files/Adams_Peter_Pan-200x200.png)<span class="lightbluebold">The
Post-Meridian Radio Players</span> are proud to present an original
full-length adaptation of J. M. Barrie’s beloved tale of <cite>Peter
Pan</cite>\! The bedtime story of all bedtime stories, the beloved tale
of the boy who won’t grow up and the girl who decides she must. The
Darling children, Wendy, John, and Michael, are whisked away to
Neverland, a place of freedom and wonderment menaced by the ever-looming
danger of the pirates and their cunning leader, Captain James Hook. Will
the Darlings ever see their parents again? Will Pan the Avenger triumph
at last over Hook? And most importantly — do you believe in fairies?

See [our Auditions page](/events/pan/auditions) to learn how you can be
a part of the show\!

We present an original adaptation of the J. M. Barrie novel by Michael
Lin, directed by Jess Viator.
