![](/sites/default/files/arisia06-sky2006.png)  
In January of 2006, <span class="lightbluebold">the Post Meridian Radio
Players</span> recreated the classic [<cite>Lights
Out</cite>](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lights_Out_%28radio_show%29)
radio drama “Chicken Heart” live at [the 2006 Arisia science-fiction
convention](http://2006.arisia.org/) in downtown Boston, performed live
with gooey and squishy sound effects. The performance was followed by a
“making-of” presentation.

A recording of this show is available from [our CD Baby
store](http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/postmeridianradioplayers).
