This Halloween PMRP is once again reaching back to ancient castles,
creeping ghosts and murderous madmen with <cite>[Tomes of Terror:
Lenore\!](/events/lenore)</cite> And to do it we’re looking at the
masters of Gothic horror\! Edgar Allan Poe\! Nathaniel Hawthorne\! Edith
Nesbit\! Wilkie Collins\!

Now is the time to write your original adaptation of a classic Gothic
tale for our upcoming Halloween show <cite>[</cite>  
](/events/lenore)

## Details\!

  - We are looking for original adaptations of existing stories. Edgar
    Allan Poe strongly encouraged, but other Gothic horror writers are
    accepted.
  - The original stories should be in the public domain.
  - Adaptations with some revisions, such as gender-swapping the
    characters, changing the time period or location can be submitted. 
    (in fact we encourage female characters in your story\!)
  - We are not looking for pastiches or Poe-like stories
  - While some humor is fine, we are not looking for parodies.
  - The following stories are not available: “The Cask of Amontillado”,
    “The Fall of the House of Usher”, “The Masque of the Red Death”,
    “The Raven”, “The Murders in the Rue Morgue”, “The Purloined
    Letter” and “The Mystery of Marie Rogêt”.

## Specifics:

  - Submissions should be completed scripts in radio drama format.  This
    performance uses live Foley performers as well, so remember the
    sound effects.
  - Your play should run 22-25 minutes.
  - There is a maximum of 5 voice characters on mic at any one time (we
    have a small stage).  Crowd scenes do not count towards this limit.
  - We understand that this is a horror show, but gore and adult content
    should be kept to a reasonable level.  So PG-13 is fine, R is okay
    but NC-17 is right out.
  - Previously produced material is allowed, providing the author
    retains the rights to the script.
  - Up to two submissions from the same author(s) are allowed.
  - Scripts previously submitted to <cite>[Tomes of Terror:
    Nevermore](/events/nevermore)</cite> may be re-submitted.
  - Scripts may be revised as needed for the performance and PMRP
    retains all performance rights including director choice, casting
    and sound design.

Scripts need to be received **on or before June 30th**.   Notifications
about acceptance will go out by July 15th.     

## Submissions

Please send your completed script to Chris DeKalb at
cdkboston@earthlink.net.    PDF format preferred although we will accept
`.doc` files as well. 

**Put your contact information in the script\!  **  

## The Fine Print

If accepted PMRP pays $50 for the performance rights for the script.  In
addition PMRP retains rights to record and sell recordings of the
performance, but all other script rights (including ownership) remain
with the author(s).   As noted previously scripts may be revised as
needed for the performance and PMRP retains all performance rights
including director choice, casting and sound design.

Rights payments will be sent on or before November 15th, 2017.

PMRP is not required to accept any submissions.  In the unfortunate
occurrence that none of the submissions are accepted Tomes of Terror:
Lenore\! will be performed with pre-existing scripts instead.

We look forward to seeing your work\!
