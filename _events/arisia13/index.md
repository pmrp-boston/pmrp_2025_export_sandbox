**Gort, Klaatu Barada Nikto\!**  
From the long-running [<cite>Lux Radio
Theater</cite>](http://www.radiohof.org/adventuredrama/luxradio.html)
comes this adaptation of the [classic 1951 science-fiction
film](http://www.tcm.com/this-month/article/145423%7C0/The-Day-the-Earth-Stood-Still.html).
Originally airing on January 4th, 1954, this production featured Michael
Rennie reprising his performance of Klaatu. <cite>Lux Radio
Theater</cite> was famous for producing radio adaptations of major
motion pictures, often with the original lead actors.

This adaptation of Edmund H. North's original film script was written by
Milton Geiger. The film script itself was based on the Harry Bates short
story “Farewell to the Master”.
