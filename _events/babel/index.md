## Performance Dates

Friday, June 3, 2016 @ 8:00pm  
Saturday, June 4, 2016 @ 2:00pm (matinée)  
Saturday, June 4, 2016 @ 8:00pm  
([The performance at Arisia](/events/arisia16) is at 10pm on Saturday,
January 16.)

If you would like to reserve your spot (not required), you can do so on
[our Reservations page](/tickets).

All performances will be at [Unity
Somerville](http://www.unitygreaterboston.org/) (just off of College Ave
near Davis Square)\!

-----

![](/sites/default/files/j2b-small.png)In June, we will reprise
[Arisia](http://www.arisia.org/) science-fiction convention, the
Post-Meridian Radio Players will reprise the Gender-Swapped <cite>Star
Trek</cite>: episode [we performed](/events/arisia16) at the Arisia
science-fiction convention in January, “Journey to Babel”\! The
Enterprise finds itself host to a number of bickering diplomats -
including Spock's parents. When an alien dignitary is murdered, the
prime suspect is Spock's mother\! The crew must unravel the mystery as
an unidentified vessel attacks.
