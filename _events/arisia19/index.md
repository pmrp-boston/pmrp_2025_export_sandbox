The Post-Meridian Radio Players will have *two* performances at
[the 2019 Arisia science-fiction convention](https://arisia.org/). We
will continue our tradition of presenting gender-swapped <cite>Star
Trek</cite> episodes, this time performing “The Enterprise Incident”. In
addition we will perform a reprise of our original adaptation of
Sherlock Holmes and Doctor Watson in “The Final Problem”.

More details will be provided shortly.

The performances will be at time slots to be determined during Arisia,
which is the weekend of January 18-21, 2019. (A convention membership or
day pass is required. If you can’t attend at the con, watch for our
reprise of “The Enterprise Incident” in the spring\!)
