The Post-Meridian Radio Players are thrilled to announce that they are
seeking actors to perform in their next gender-swapped <cite>Star Trek
episode</cite> — “The Enterprise Incident”\!

In this classic D.C. Fontana episode, Captain Kirk mysteriously becomes
apparently irrational and unstable, steering the ship into Romulan
territory to the horror of the crew\! The Enterprise is quickly
captured, and Spock must negotiate with the captivating Romulan
Commander\!

Auditions will take place Wednesday, October 3rd at a private Somerville
residence. Please email Caitlin Mason at my.london.sun@gmail.com to set
up an audition appointment.

Before your audition, you’ll want to look at the audition sides, which
you can [view and download
here](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1EWtggwSlm2O78sohx_IclVkTEYEhSZAM).
It will be helpful if you can bring a copy of the sides with you to your
audition.

Performances will be on one day TBD of Martin Luther King Day weekend
(January 18-21) at the Boston science fiction convention
[Arisia](http://www.arisia.org/), and a one-weekend run in June 2019
(exact dates TBD).

The roles we are looking to fill are:

  - **Ms. Sulu** (Female): Senior helmswoman and third officer on the
    ship, Sulu is "is at heart a swashbuckler out of the 18th century."
    *(please note, we are seeking an **Asian/Asian-American** actress
    for this role.)*
  - **The Romulan Commander** (Male): Featured guest role. The commander
    of the Romulan flagship -- An homme fatale who engages in an
    extended flirtation with Spock, attempting to seduce her to the
    Romulan side.
  - **Sub-Commander Tal** (Female): The Commander's second-in-command,
    who carries out the initial Romulan discussions with the Enterprise
    and later in the episode leads the Romulan charge to chase down the
    ship.
  - **Romulans, Various** (Female): We will cast likely 2 actors to
    cover various smaller roles of guards and officers who cause trouble
    for our heroes.

We hope to hear from you soon\! Remember to email Caitlin Mason at
my.london.sun@gmail.com to secure an audition spot, or with any
questions\!
