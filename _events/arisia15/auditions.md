The Post-Meridian Radio Players are thrilled to announce that they are
seeking actors to perform in their next gender-swapped <cite>Star Trek:
The Original Series episode</cite> — “Space Seed”\! In “Space Seed”, the
Enterprise discovers an ancient spaceship carrying genetically enhanced
superwomen from late 20th century Earth, and their enigmatic warlord
leader: Khan Noonien Singh.

Auditions will take place Sunday, November 16th from 8-10pm, and Monday,
November 17th from 7-10pm at a private Somerville residence. Please
email Caitlin Mason at my.london.sun@gmail.com to set up an audition
appointment.

Performances will be on either Friday January 16th or Saturday January
17th at the Boston science fiction convention
[Arisia](http://2015.arisia.org), a one-weekend run in May/June 2015
(exact dates TBD) and at Boston ComicCon in summer 2015 (exact date
TBD).

The roles we are looking to fill, including character descriptions, are
listed below:

  - <span class="standout">Lt. Michael McGivers</span> (Male) (“My
    profession is historian, and when I find a specimen from the past
    alive, it is sheer delight to examine her mind.”): Knowledgeable and
    competent ship’s historian, who falls in love with Khan Noonien
    Singh against his better judgment and must wrangle incompatible
    loyalties and complex romantic power dynamics. Featured guest role.
  - <span class="standout">Khan Noonien Singh</span> (Female —
    preference for Indian or Latina actresses, or other actresses of
    color) (“The trip is over. The battle begins again. Only this time
    it's not a world we win. It's a universe”). A genetically engineered
    human with augmented physical and analytical abilities, she was a
    powerful leader on 1990's Earth, and hopes to regain her place in
    the sun. Her attempts to hide her identity and gain control of the
    ship form the main plot of the episode. Featured co-star.
  - <span class="standout">Joaquin</span> (female): Genetically
    engineered human, a follower of Singh. Hits and threatens Uhura
    during the attempted takeover of the ship.

We hope to hear from you soon\! Remember to email Caitlin Mason at
my.london.sun@gmail.com to secure an audition spot, or with any
questions\!
