***KHAAAAAAAAAN\!***  
Actress-turned-Director Mindy Klenoff (“The Do-Nothing Detective”) is
again presenting a <span class="lightbluebold">gender-swapped</span>
interpretation of a classic <cite>Star Trek</cite> episode. This time
she’s showing us a new perspective on “Space Seed”, the episode that
introduced the genetically engineered ~~superman~~ superwoman Khan.

The show will be performed at the Arisia Sci-Fi convention
([www.arisia.org](http://www.arisia.org/)) in January over MLK weekend.
See below for more information (including cast and venue details).  
![](/sites/default/files/spaceseedposter.jpg)
