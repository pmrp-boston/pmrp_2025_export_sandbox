-----

<span class="lightbluebold">The Post-Meridian Radio Players</span> are
proud to present their sixth annual Summer Radio program with
<cite>Revenge of the Super Sleuths</cite>: a trilogy of spine-tingling
murder mysteries\!

The Trio of Terrific Truthseekers will tantalize the stage of
Responsible Grace Church in Somerville for six perfomances\!

-----

### <span class="lightblue">Lady Molly of Scotland Yard</span>  
<span class="small">in</span>  
<span class="smallcaps">The Irish Tweed Coat</span>

**Directed by Caitlin Mason**  

Lady Molly must outwit the Italian Mafia\! Molly sends her assistant
Mary undercover to retrieve an Irish tweed coat from two Sicilians. This
coat is all that stands between an innocent man and the hangman's
noose\!

### <span class="lightblue">Sherlock Holmes</span>  
<span class="small">in</span>  
<span class="smallcaps">The Adventure of the Speckled Band</span>

**Directed by Jeremy Holstein**  

Sherlock Holmes and Doctor Watson are visited by a young woman whose
sister died under mysterious circumstances. The case leads them to a run
down estate over run by Indian animals where they must solve a locked
room murder mystery before it is too late\! Widely regarded as the best
of the Sir Arthur Conan Doyle short stories.
