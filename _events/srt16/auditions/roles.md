For [SRT16: Revenge of the Super Sleuths](/events/srt16) we have
openings for both Foley Artists and Voice Actors. This page describes
the voice parts. For more information about the auditions process
(including links to the form to sign up for a slot), see [our Auditions
page](/events/srt16/auditions), and for descriptions of the stories, see
[the event page](/events/srt16).  

## Hercule Poirot in <cite>Murder is a Private Affair</cite>

(Please note Inspector Poirot, Abigail Fletcher and Inspector Stevens
are pre-cast.)

  - **Cornelia Baldwin**  
    A bitter old woman who is deeply suspicious of her younger family
    members.
  - **Olivia Hammond**  
    A shifty and self-serving actress looking to make a quick buck.
  - **Henry Baldwin**  
    Dashing and handsome, he is Cornelia's nephew and Olivia's former
    lover.
  - **Frances Baldwin**  
    Henry's very nervous and insecure wife.
  - **Laurence Baldwin**  
    Cornelia's mild-mannered and romantic son who has fallen in love
    with Olivia.
  - **Jean Baldwin**\* (Non-speaking)  
    Cornelia's daughter who thwarts her mother's controlling nature.
  - **Williams**  
    An elderly manservant at the Baldwin Manor.
  - **Marie**\*  
    Cornelia's maid.

\* The roles of "Jean Baldwin" and "Marie" will be played by the same
actress.  

## Lady Molly of Scotland Yard in <cite>The Irish Tweed Coat</cite>

  - **Lady Molly** (female, lead)  
    A brilliant detective who works for the Scotland Yard Female
    Department. Strongly opinionated and very proud of having earned her
    position, she takes orders only from the Chief.
  - **Mary** (female, lead)  
    Lady Molly's private secretary. Fiercy loyal to Lady Molly, to the
    point of obsession. She is our everywoman, and narrates the tale.
  - **Emily** (female, lead)  
    The young assistant to Lady Molly and Mary. Usually a housekeeper,
    she is swept up into the investigation and is only too willing to
    help.
  - **Mr. Shuttleworth** (male, lead)  
    The client, and father to the accused. Dogged in his devotion to
    both his son and his fiance, his loyalty blinds him to the truth of
    their flaws.
  - **Mrs. Taggert** (female)  
    Mr. Shuttleworth's fiance, a widowed Italian who owns a boarding
    house. Despite Mr. Shuttleworth's devotion, and her affection for
    his son, she is dreadfully afraid of the Mafia and her fear might
    give the game away.
  - **Piatti** (male)  
    A elder member of the Mafia, he is not above using intimidation to
    get what he wants.
  - **Officer** (male/maybe female)  
    A member of the Hungarian police force. All business.
  - **Clerk/Porter/Maid**  
    All staff members of the Hotel Hungaria.

## Sherlock Holmes in <cite>The Adventure of the Speckled Band</cite>

(Please note Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson are pre-cast)

  - **Helen** (female, lead)  
    Sister to the murder victim, she is a young woman in the prime of
    her life, yet old before her time because of circumstance and
    tragedy. She has come to consult Sherlock Holmes about the
    mysterious circumstances which led to the death of her sister.
  - **Julia** (female)  
    The victim, and sister to Helen. Like Helen she too is in the prime
    of her life, but tragedy has scarred her.
  - **Roylott** (male)  
    Step-father to Helen and Julia, and owner of the estate where Julia
    died. A violent and explosive temper is his most significant
    character trait.
  - **Driver** (Male/either?)  
    A carriage driver who ferries Holmes and Watson to Stoke Moran.
