## Performance Dates

Friday, June 5, 2015 @ 8:00pm  
Saturday, June 6, 2015 @ 2:00pm (matinée)  
Saturday, June 6, 2015 @ 8:00pm

All performances will be at [Unity
Somerville](http://www.unitygreaterboston.org/) (just off of College Ave
near Davis Square)\!

![](/sites/default/files/spaceseedbanner_0.jpg)  
***KHAAAAAAAAAN\!***

If you didn't get a chance to see our **gender-swapped** interpretation
of the classic Star Trek episode "Space Seed" at Arisia ’15, you have
another opportunity coming up\! We'll be performing the show again at
Unity Somerville on June 5-6.

This was the episode that introduced genetically engineered ~~superman~~
superwoman Khan, who came back to menace the Enterprise in <cite>Star
Trek II: The Wrath of Khan</cite>.
