The Post-Meridian Radio Players are thrilled to announce that they are
seeking actors to perform in their next gender-swapped <cite>Star
Trek</cite> episode — “Amok Time”\!

In this episode, Spock suffers through ponn farr — a Vulcan mating urge
— and must return to the planet Vulcan to marry her betrothed.

Auditions will take place Thursday, October 5, at a private Somerville
residence. Please email Caitlin Mason at my.london.sun@gmail.com to set
up an audition appointment.

Performances will be on one day TBD of Martin Luther King Day weekend
(January 12-15 at the Boston science fiction convention Arisia), and a
one-weekend run in June 2018 (exact dates TBD).

The roles we are looking to fill are:

  - **T’Pau** (Male): A powerful Vulcan patriarch who presides over the
    Koon-ut-kal-if-fee ceremony in which Spock is forced to fight
    Captain Kirk for the hand of her husband-to-be, T’Pring. He is the
    only person ever to turn down a seat on the Federation Council.
  - **T’Pring** (Male): Spock’s betrothed. T’Pring cleverly orchestrates
    a fight between Spock and Kirk for his own reasons.
  - **Stonn** (Female): Bodyguard of T’Pring.
  - **Admiral Komack** (Female): A Federation official.

We hope to hear from you soon\! Remember to email Caitlin Mason at
my.london.sun@gmail.com to secure an audition spot, or with any
questions\!
