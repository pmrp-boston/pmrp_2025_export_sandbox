## Foley/FX Artists

Live sound effects are an important part of our shows. Interviews for
Foley/FX positions will be conducted on the same nights as cast
auditions. So be sure to [**sign up for an audition
slot**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/embeddedform?formkey=dFluenAyaUhUZTYycnpOak8yMTJzTlE6MA),
even if you are just there for a Foley interview. On the night of your
slot, you will sit down with one of our regular Foley/FX artists and be
interviewed.

Things that will be helpful, but are not required, include:

  - Previous experience as a Foley/FX artist, either with PMRP or
    another organization
  - Previous experience with creating or designing live sound effects
  - Experience with rhythm instruments (drums, percussion, etc.)
  - Creativity (useful for developing ways to create sounds)
  - Ability to work within a team
  - Availability (Foley/FX artists have a more extensive schedule than
    the rest of the cast and crew)
  - Enthusiasm\!
