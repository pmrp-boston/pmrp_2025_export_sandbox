-----

<span class="lightbluebold">The Post-Meridian Radio Players</span> are
proud to present their fourth annual Summer Radio program with
<cite>Super Sleuths</cite>: a trilogy of spine-tingling murder
mysteries\!

The Trio of Terrific Truthseekers will tantalize the stage of
Responsible Grace Church in Somerville for six performances\!

### <span class="lightblue">Lady Molly of Scotland Yard</span>  
<span class="small">in</span>  
<span class="redbold">THE NINESCORE MYSTERY</span>

**Directed by Caitlin Mason**  

In 1910, Baroness Orczy (<cite>The Scarlet Pimpernel</cite>) introduced
one of the first female detectives into the world of mystery fiction. In
this tale, Lady Molly is given a chance to prove herself to her
superiors by solving the Ninescore murder case.
