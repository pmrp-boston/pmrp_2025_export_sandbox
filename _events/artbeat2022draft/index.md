<big>As part of
[ArtBeat 2022](https://somervilleartscouncil.org/artbeat/2022) in July,
PMRP invites you on a walking tour like no other with **Strange Tales of
Davis Square**, a collection of odd and uncanny stories brought to you
by some of PMRP's finest writers and voice actors that will leave you
looking at parts of Davis Square in a totally new light\! What message
does the Powderhouse Ghost send from beyond? What strange creature once
roamed where the farmers market now stands? Which building has a
shocking past? Take the tour and find out\! Beginning July 11th, you
will have two ways to listen. If you have the ECHOES app ([for
Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=xyz.echoes.android)
or [for
iPhone](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/echoes-interactive-sound-walks/id1021511722))
open on your phone, doing a search for **Strange Tales of Davis Square**
will open up the tour map, and as you go to each glowing point on the
map you will hear the story of that location\! If you don't have the
ECHOES app (or if you’d like to re-listen to any of our stories), that's
no problem — just come back to this page for links to each story,
credits and much more\!</big>

If you don’t have the Echoes app, we have [a map of the walking tour on
Google
Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/1/edit?mid=1C19BLMg6qsuOhk2qRCKCW7fsYjqJyO0&usp=sharing)
to show where each story takes place. You can also scroll down to the
bottom of this page to see the map. (But feel free to listen to them
below if you don’t feel like taking the tour).
