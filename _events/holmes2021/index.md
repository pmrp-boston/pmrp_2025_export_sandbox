![](/sites/default/files/HolmesIcon.png)The Post-Meridian Radio Players
are proud to present [The Summer of Mystery](/events/summerofmystery)\!
Three free Zoom performances, the last weekends in June, July and
August\!

In July we have **The Adventure of the Bruce-Partington Plans**, one of
only two adventures to feature Sherlock’s older, smarter brother
Mycroft. Holmes and Watson investigate the theft of the plans of
England’s greatest naval weapon, the Bruce-Partington submarine\! The
fate of England itself hangs in the balance\!

-----

## Performances

Thursday, July 29, 2021 at 8pm  
Saturday, July 31, 2021 at 8pm
