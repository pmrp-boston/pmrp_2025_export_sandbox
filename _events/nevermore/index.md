**Performances:** October 24, 25, 29, 30, and 31 and November 1, 2014.

-----

![](/sites/default/files/tomes5_mdlogo.png)**There’s one due date you
can *never* escape\!** —The Librarian

<span class="lightbluebold">The Post-Meridian Radio Players</span> are
bringing one of America's most respected authors to our stage this
Hallowe’en as we present <cite>Tomes of Terror: Nevermore</cite>.

Three tales of Edgar Allan Poe will grace our stage—and your ears—this
Fall, including adaptations of his classic stories <cite>The Cask of
Amontillado</cite> and <cite>The Fall of the House of Usher</cite>. And
we’ve got a little extra treat as well to set the mood\!

storefront](https://pmrp.bandcamp.com/)\!</big>

### The Masque of the Red Death

Adapted by A. Lehrmitt and directed by Justus Perry and A. Lehrmitt  
A prince attempts to wait out the horrors of a plague by shutting
himself, with many other nobles, in his abbey and distracting them by
luxury and celebration from the suffering beyond the walls. Will Death
so easily be cheated?
