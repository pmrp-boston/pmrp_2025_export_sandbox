On Friday, February 11, 2022 at 9:30pm, we will perform our
gender-swapped adaptation of the classic <cite>Star Trek</cite> episode
“City on the Edge of Forever” over Zoom. In this episode, originally
written by sci-fi icon Harlan Ellison, Captain Jane T. Kirk and Ms.
Spock must follow a delirious Dr. McCoy into the 1930s before she can
alter the course of history. Will Kirk make the necessary sacrifice to
save the past and secure the future?

We’ll be streaming this LIVE at 9:30pm at [**this
link**](https://youtu.be/h2e3XT7rb24) on [PMRP's YouTube
channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/postmeridianradio)\! We hope you
can join us\!
