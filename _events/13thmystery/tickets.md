## <span id="performances"></span>Performances

[“The Thirteenth Mystery”](/events/13thmystery) will be performed on the
following dates:

  - Friday, July 19, 2024, at 8:00pm [in Davis Square](/venues/204elm)
  - Saturday, July 20, 2024, at 8:00pm [in Davis Square](/venues/204elm)
  - Friday, July 26, 2024, at 7:30pm [in Watertown](/venues/mca)
  - Saturday, July 27, 2024, at 7:30pm [in Watertown](/venues/mca)
  - Sunday, July 28, 2024, at 2:00pm [in Watertown](/venues/mca)
    (matinée)

Note that the Watertown shows start at 7:30 (or 2:00 for the matinée),
while the Davis Square shows start at 8:00.
