This year, Hallowe’en comes alive\!

## Featuring

![Night of the Living Dead](/sites/default/files/notld_header.jpg)

<span class="lightbluebold">THE POST-MERIDIAN RADIO PLAYERS</span>
present a brand new radio adaptation of the cult zombie flick that
started it all: the classic tale of a group trying to survive when the
Dead refuse to die\!

Adapted for radio and directed by Jay Sekora (*THEM\!*)  
Original film script by George A. Romero and John A. Russo
