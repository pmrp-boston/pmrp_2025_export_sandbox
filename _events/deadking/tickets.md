## <span id="performances"></span>Performances

[“Night of the Living Dead” with “The King in Yellow”](/events/deadking)
will be performed on the following dates:

  - Friday, October 20, 2023, at 8:00pm, at [Guild
    Church](/venues/204elm) (formerly Boston Brunch Church) near Davis
    Square, Somerville
  - Saturday, October 21, 2023, at 2:00pm (matinée\!), at [Guild
    Church](/venues/204elm) near Davis Square, Somerville  
    <i>The October 21 matinée is pay-what-you-can.<i>
  - Saturday, October 21, 2023, at 8:00pm, at [Guild
    Church](/venues/204elm) near Davis Square, Somerville
  - Friday, October 27, 2023, at 7:30pm, at [Mosesian Center for the
    Arts](/venues/mca) in Watertown
  - Saturday, October 28, 2023, at 7:30pm, at [Mosesian Center for the
    Arts](/venues/mca) in Watertown
  - Sunday, October 29, 2023, at 1:30pm (matinée\!), at [Mosesian Center
    for the Arts](/venues/mca) in Watertown

Note that the Watertown shows start at 7:30 (or 1:30 for the matinée),
while the Davis Square shows start at 8:00 (or 2:00 for the matinée).

## Tickets and reservations — first weekend

<span class="small">*(Scroll down for the second weekend at
MCA.)*</span>  
You may buy tickets for **the Somerville performances** advance on-line
through [Brown Paper Tickets](https://deadking.bpt.me/):  
[![](https://www.brownpapertickets.com/g/6/BPT_buy_tickets_large.png)](https://deadking.bpt.me/)  
**$20 / $15 Students & Seniors 62+**  
(not including BPT’s fees)  
or  
You may reserve tickets (again, only for the Somerville performances)
using [this reservation form](https://forms.gle/MP5uJeHTNzLyYQpNA) and
pay for them at the door:  
<big><big><big>[Ticket Reservation
Form](https://forms.gle/MP5uJeHTNzLyYQpNA)</big></big></big>  
(If you want to choose your own price for the Saturday matinée, the
reservation form is how to do that; just make a reservation for that
performance and pay what you wish at the door.)
