## <span id="bios">Cast and Crew Biographies:</span>

<span id="Simone Agha">**Simone Agha**</span> (Foley Coordinator,
Foley/TKiY) is thrilled to be once again making noise for a PMRP
production. This is her \*mumbles\*th show stomping and \<unintelligible
noise\> with PMRP.

<span id="Eli Baker">**Eli Baker**</span> (Louis/TKiY) is coming back
for his second year at PMRP after his role as Ensemble in last year’s
Halloween performance of Three O’Clock. Outside of theatre, they enjoy
playing video games, watching horror movies, and playing with their
cats.

<span id="Hannah Baker">**Hannah Baker**</span> (Director and
Co-Playwright/TKiY) most recently directed The Cherry Orchard for
Theatre@First and was most recently seen in PMRP as an alien henchman in
Plan 8 From the Outer Ether. She definitely did not stage this play as a
dark summoning ritual.

<span id="Alex Bazis">**Alex Bazis**</span> (Ensemble/NotLD) is thrilled
for his second show with PMRP\! He loves old-time radio and horror, and
wishes the word 'ghoul' was used more in everyday conversation. He also
writes and directs audio dramas through 104 WPR Metro. Check out
Wolfwhistle, a surreal horror drama about radio hosts in the 1930s\!

<span id="Elly Berke">**Elly Berke**</span> (Barbra/NotLD) is happy to
be joining her second PMRP production. It’s been a real scream\! She
“eeks” out a living in public education and just set up her
professional voice over business\! Love to loyal friends and fam who
support all my projects.

<span id="Mollie Bourne">**Mollie Bourne**</span> (Connie/TKiY) is in
her second show with PMRP but her first show with a voice acting role.
When not involved in shows, she loves to read and bake.

<span id="Madison Canfora">**Madison Canfora**</span> (Radio
Announcer/NotLD) is new to the city and excited to be performing in her
first Boston area show\!

<span id="Jaimie Carlson">**Jaimie Carlson**</span> (Foley/NotLD) is new
to PMRP and theater, unless you count a fourth-wall breaking performance
in the fourth-grade play (translation: she wandered offstage.) Jaimie is
an engineer from Delaware who enjoys writing trivia tournaments and
reading an ungodly amount of sci-fi short stories, when not wrangling
robots.

<span id="Caroline DeBrota">**Caroline DeBrota**</span> (Jessie,
Reporter/NotLD) is back for her second PMRP show after being seen in
Metropolis this spring\! This summer, she played Lady Stutfield in A
Woman of No Importance with Dragonfly Players, which was a "very, very"
good experience. She's delighted to share the stage with the love of her
life, Shawyoun\! Enjoy the show\!

<span id="Sara Dion">**Sara Dion**</span> (The Director/TKiY) is excited
to be back at PMRP for another show. Sara has previously performed in
several PMRP productions, such as "Santa Claus Conquers the Martians,"
"Tomes of Terror: Campfire Tales," and "Confidence Confidante." She
would like to thank her spooky black cat, Snuggle, for all of the
emotional support.

<span id="Sue Downing">**Sue Downing**</span> (Ensemble/NotLD) is voice
acting for a third Halloween show with PMRP. Sue also directed on behalf
of PMRP for their sponsorship of "Family Business" by Tegan Garon in the
2023 Boston Theater Marathon XXV earlier this year.

<span id="Mrs. Kitty  Drexel">**Mrs. Kitty Drexel**</span> (Foley/NotLD)
\[she/her/we\] is a disabled, queer performing artist and critic living
with Brachial Plexus Palsy. Recent credits include Scientia Dei in
Quorum Boston’s production of the Ordo Virtutum and Futura in PMRP’s
Metropolis. She is the Queen Geek at the New England Theatre Geek.
Abortions are healthcare. Immigrants are people. Black Lives Matter.  
Mrs. Drexel would also like to thank you for looking damn fine tonight.
\*wink\* @newengland\_theatregeek

<span id="Joev Dubach">**Joev Dubach**</span> (Tom/NotLD) is happy to be
in his twenty-eighth show with the PMRP, making a moon's worth of molten
fiction. You can join Joev for improvisational free fitness hilarity
with November Project Boston ( https://november-project.com/boston/ )
any time, as long as that time is MWF 6:30am. Just show up\!

<span id="Peter Duerst">**Peter Duerst**</span> (Ensemble/NotLD) is
thrilled to be in his first production with PMRP\! You may have seen him
in August acting with Theatre@First or many moons ago when he acted with
the MIT Shakespeare Ensemble. He hopes you enjoy the show\!

<span id="Mare Freed">**Mare Freed**</span> (Helen/NotLD) has vowed to
never use the words "thrilled," "delighted," or the phrase "when not
onstage" in a theatre bio again. She's the current Managing Director of
PMRP, and she owes her joy to her forever-love, Jay Sekora. 

<span id="Alex Gorowara">**Alex Gorowara**</span> (Ben/TKiY) is excited
to be back in theatre for the first time since college\! Long-time PMRP
listener, first time PMRP actor. Enjoys tabletop gaming, LARPing, and
singing in his spare time.

<span id="Sally Lahoud ">**Sally Lahoud**</span> (House manager —
Somerville) is a Lebanese professional organizer, dog walker, and
student of mental health counseling. She is new to PMRP but a big fan
for some time\!

<span id="Chloe Melville">**Chloe Melville**</span> (The Technical
Director/TKiY, sound designer) is new to PMRP, but is excited for their
first role in a radio show\! Their last show was the Dream Role Players
production of Twelfth Night where they played the Captain and a Servant
(aka Waldo). Chloe also enjoys playing D\&D, hiking, road-tripping, and
photography.

<span id="Patrick Neal">**Patrick Neal**</span> (Alec/TKiY) is excited
for his first show with PMRP\! He has previously appeared in The Cherry
Orchard and She Kills Monsters with Theatre@First. He would like to use
this space to recommend you try peanut butter M\&Ms - they're much
better than ordinary peanut ones. (Actor received no payment for this
bio)

<span id="Ted Overholt">**Ted Overholt**</span> (Harry/NotLD) is
spending the year without a bio for tax reasons

<span id="Elizabeth Ross">**Elizabeth Ross**</span> (The Stage
Manager/TKiY) is excited to be performing again with PMRP with whom she
appeared as Auteur (Plan 8 from the Outer Ether), Sub-Commander Tal
(Star Trek: The Enterprise Incident) and Kate Warne (Confidence
Confidant). Her other work includes Malvolio (Twelfth Night) and
Touchstone (As You Like It) with Dream Role Players, Charlotta (The
Cherry Orchard) and Lussurioso (The Revenger’s Tragedy) with
Theater@First.

<span id="Jay Sekora">**Jay Sekora**</span> (Director and adapter/NotLD)
has worked with PMRP as an actor (“The Tell-Tale Heart”, “Junkyard”,
“The Birds”) and sound engineer as well as a director and adapter.
He's directed PMRP's adaptations of “THEM\!”, “Metropolis”, and
“Filibus”. He thanks his beloved partner Mare for (among many other
things) getting him back into theater.

<span id="Shawyoun Shaidani">**Shawyoun Shaidani**</span> (Johnny/NotLD)
is excited to return to PMRP after having appeared in “Metropolis” in
the spring (when he also, coincidentally, played a punk). He is happy to
share the stage with Caroline for their 8th show together\! :) He thanks
the cast/crew for this experience, and the audience for supporting
theater\!

<span id="Amelia  Smith">**Amelia Smith**</span> (Foley/TKiY) is
delighted to be performing for the first time with PMRP. Her most recent
theatrical project was directing an audiodrama (Congratulations, Dear
Stranger) with Hear Me Out Productions, and she’s excited to be
branching into live radio plays. Outside of theater, she enjoys spoiling
her two cats, playing D\&D, and studying nuclear fusion.

<span id="Jacob Sommer">**Jacob Sommer**</span> (Ensemble/NotLD) is back
on stage with PMRP, though some of his hair and beard have gone
suspiciously walkabout. He makes a top-notch chicken marsala, sings in a
perfect Kermit voice, and has a perfect alibi for last Tuesday. Warning:
has willingly fed chowhounds.

<span id="Joye Thaller">**Joye Thaller**</span> (Judy/NotLD) is a
long-time PMRP veteran, and has been involved since the very first Tomes
of Terror in 2006. She was last seen on stage in the PMRP Boston Theatre
Marathon show, "Family Business." She also directed "La Siguanaba," part
of the 2019 Tomes of Terror.

<span id="Pooja Usgaonkar">**Pooja Usgaonkar**</span> (Producer, Graphic
Designer, Co-Playwright) is new to PMRP's crew and wants to thank
everyone for their hard work on this show\! Most recent acting credits
include Festival@First: Myths and Legends, Boston Theater Marathon,
Dangerous Liaisons (Cecile) with Headtrick Theatre, and The Secret
Garden (Ayah) with The Concord Players. When she isn't at her day job as
a software engineer, she enjoys playing saxophone with FlyBy Brass Band.

<span id="Jaclyn Wilson">**Jaclyn Wilson**</span> (Stage manager) is
excited to be back wrangling the cast of this year's Halloween show. She
identifies strongly with the SM character in The King in Yellow, and is
glad that all eldritch and zombie horrors are contained to the stage for
both shows... or are they?

<span id="Ray Zaslow">**Ray Zaslow**</span> (Sound engineer) is new to
PMRP's team, but he met them back in April, under equally terrifying
circumstances: through a hypercapitalist dystopia that heavily inspired
retrofuturist sci-fi. In his free time, Ray enjoys long walks around
Allston, listening to whatever music he can get his hands on, and
embroidering patches for his favorite jacket.
