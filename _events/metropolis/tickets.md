## <span id="performances"></span>Performances

[<cite>Metropolis</cite>](/events/metropolis) will be performed on the
following dates:

  - Thursday, April 20, at 8pm at [Guild Church](/venues/204elm)
    (formerly Boston Brunch Church) near Davis Square, Somerville
  - Friday, April 21, at 8pm, at [Guild Church](/venues/204elm) near
    Davis Square, Somerville
  - Saturday, April 22, at 2pm (matinée\!), at [Guild
    Church](/venues/204elm) near Davis Square, Somerville  
    *This matinée performance is pay-what-you-can.*
  - Saturday, April 22, at 8pm, at [Guild Church](/venues/204elm) near
    Davis Square, Somerville
  - Friday, April 28, at 7:30pm, at [Mosesian Center for the
    Arts](/venues/mca) in Watertown
  - Saturday, April 29, at 7:30pm, at [Mosesian Center for the
    Arts](/venues/mca) in Watertown

Note that the Watertown shows start at 7:30, while the Davis Square
shows start at 8:00 (except for the 2:00 matinée).

in advance on-line through [Brown Paper
Tickets](https://bpt.me/5750059):  
[![](https://www.brownpapertickets.com/g/6/BPT_buy_tickets_large.png)](https://bpt.me/5750059)

(not including BPT’s fees)

You may reserve tickets (again, only for the Somerville performances)
using [this reservation form](https://forms.gle/ZKcyhGLAxvBFdLZH9) and
pay for them at the door:  
<big><big><big>[Ticket Reservation

reservation form is how to do that; just make a reservation for that
performance and pay what you wish at the door.)
