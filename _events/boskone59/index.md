![](/sites/default/files/holmes-head-120.png)![](/sites/default/files/partington2-120_0.png)On
Saturday, February 19, [PMRP will be presenting Sherlock Holmes in
“Sherlock Holmes in the Adventure of the Bruce Partington Plans” at
Boskone 59](https://schedule.boskone.org/id/10535). A Boskone membership
is needed to attend or view the performance, which will be presented in
person in the hotel (in the Harbor I room) and streamed over Boskone’s
streaming service for virtual con attendees.

In this performance, Sherlock's brother Mycroft hires Holmes and Watson
to investigate a crime that threatens the security both of the crown and
of England\!  

## Performance

[Saturday, February 19, 2022,
at 7:00pm](https://schedule.boskone.org/id/10535)  
(in Harbor I at Boskone, or streaming with a Boskone online
membership.)
